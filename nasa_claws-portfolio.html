The CLAWS Interface

I've joined SEDS@UM as a user experience specialist developing a mixed reality UI (CLAWS) on the Microsoft HoloLens for the NASA SUITS Challenge. We are using the CLAWS system to prototype mission related modules for the NASA X-Hab Challenge, as well as a dashboard for Mission Control. We have faculty sponsorship and are 1 of 10 teams invited to present to NASA onsite at the Johnson Space Center in Houston. I'm focusing on user research with some input on the design. We're primarily utilizing expert reviews, user interviews and human-in-the-loop testing, literature reviews and statistical analysis.


SEDS@UM - Students for the Exploration and Development of Space at University of Michigan
CLAWS - Compact Localized Augmented Workstation, and SEDS@UM subgroup
