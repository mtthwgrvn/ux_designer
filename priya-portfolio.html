Priya Indian Cuisine Website Redesign

Priya is an award winning local favorite for Indian food specializing in Tandoor and South Indian flavors. Recently, it came to my attention that they are in need of a website redesign.


I have re-envisioned this website as a single page with easy to navigate anchor links, and a fresh and appealing look. The full menu is an overlay of the main page. Dish images makes use of slideshow carousels. I've drafted a mockup of my design which you can see in more detail on Behance.
